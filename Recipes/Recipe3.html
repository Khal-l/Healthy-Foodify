Soy and vegetable pita burgers


Ingredients


Olive Oil: tsp
Ground Cumin: tsp
Ground Coriander: tsp
Green Onions (thinly sliced):
Can Soybeans (drained & rinsed): g
Small Zucchini (grated):
Small Carrot (peeled & grated):
Egg (lightly beaten): g
Dried Multigrain Breadcrumbs: cup(s)
Small Wholemeal Pita Rounds (halved):
Mesclun Salad Mix: g
Medium Tomato (thinly sliced):
Reduced-Fat Hommus dip: cup(s)

Total Calories:

Method

  • Heat 1 teaspoon oil in a non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat. Add cumin, coriander and onion. Cook, stirring, for 2 to 3 minutes or until fragrant. Remove from heat. Place beans in a bowl. Mash until almost smooth.
  • Add cumin mixture, zucchini, carrot, egg and breadcrumbs to beans. Stir to combine. Using 2 tablespoons mixture per ball, roll into 8 balls. Flatten slightly. Place on a plate. Cover. Refrigerate for 15 minutes, if time permits.
  • Heat remaining oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat. Cook patties for 3 to 4 minutes each side or until golden and cooked through.
  • Fill each pita half with salad, tomato and 1 patty. Top with hommus. Serve.
